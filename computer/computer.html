How To Play

  • Each person takes a turn and draws their symbol in one of the empty squares.
  • Don't draw your symbol in a square that is already filled.
  • Once you place your mark, you can't take it back.
  • You have to make straight line: Horizontally, Vertically, Diagonally.
  • If noone will be able to make straight line, then it's Tie.
